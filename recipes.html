When you eat a snack is just as important as what you eat.

2+ hours Pre-Run

1-Hour Pre-Run

1 frozen banana
1/2 cup non-fat (Greek) yogurt
1 Tbsp cacoa powder
1/2 cup milk

< 30 minutes

1tbsp honey or jam
